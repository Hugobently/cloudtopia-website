The app today

Cloudlings is deep in development and playable today — a complete, story-driven experience rather than a prototype.

What's inside

  • 3 interactive, story-driven adventures — First Day, Beach Day and Water Day
  • 12 educational mini-games across three difficulty levels
  • Cloud House building, avatar customization and a sundrop reward loop
  • Designed for co-play: built for children, intuitive for parents
  • No ads, no in-app purchases — just safe, curated content
