The Vision

A magical world in the clouds

High above the everyday, Cloudtopia is a soft, sunlit world where little ones play, imagine, and discover how to care for the world around them. Imagination is the catalyst for every adventure.

Imagine

Character-led stories spark big imaginations, where every cloud can become a brand-new adventure.

Care

Eco-themes are woven into the play itself — clean energy, water, recycling — so caring comes naturally.

Play & Learn

Gentle, joyful learning built for ages 2–6 — social-emotional, creative and curious, never a lesson in disguise.

The App  ·  Live product

Play, imagine, care

Cloudlings — named after its four little heroes — is an interactive, animated “eco-tainment” app for children 2–6, set in the world of Cloudtopia. It’s in development on the road to release; here’s what’s inside.

Psst — sundrops are hiding all over this page. Tap them to collect, just like in the app!

1

Interactive storybooks

First Day

The cloudlings’ very first day at school. Wake them gently, serve a fluffy breakfast, and help a kind old lady clear away cloud-rocks — a cloudling transforms to solve each problem.

Kindness & helping hands

Beach Day

Bake and colour sweet puffs, cross the Rainbow Orchard, and surf the shimmering Nimbus Sea on a sun-soaked day by the water.

Keeping the sea clean

Water Day

It’s the Water Festival — but the water’s gone! Help Mr Agricloud water his farm and discover where all the water went.

Water conservation

Next up: Forest Day — already growing in the studio.

2

A world of mini-games

Dozens of bright, gentle mini-games hide inside every adventure. Tap any screen to take a closer look.

Inside the adventures: Castella’s Rockdrop Surf Sweet Puff Colour Match Whale Clean Water the Farm Mountain Melt Musical Icicles Hail Catcher Snowball Defense Forest Balloon …and more on the way
3

Build your Cloud House

Your own home in the clouds

Professor Sunbeam helps every child build and grow a Cloud House that generates clean, green energy. Tap to upgrade, decorate the rooms, and watch your little world come to life.

  • Tap to upgrade — and grow more green energy
  • Collect sundrops by playing stories and games
  • Spend them on new items, decorations and whole new houses
4

Make it yours

Customise your cloudling & your home

Make Cloudtopia truly your own. Dress up your avatar, restyle your rooms and fill your home with the things you love — every choice is a little act of imagination.

Avatar dress-up Room styling Furniture & décor Collectables
